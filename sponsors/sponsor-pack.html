About the Club

The Moorabbin Kangaroos Football Club has been a proud part of the local community since 1982. Based at Widdop Crescent Oval, Hampton East, we compete in the Southern Football and Netball League (SFNL) Division 4 and field two senior men's teams each week.

Our club is built on mateship, respect, and a love of the game. We provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for players of all backgrounds and experience levels.

Why Partner with Us?

A partnership with the Moorabbin Kangaroos connects your brand directly to the local community in Melbourne's Bayside and south-eastern suburbs. Our sponsors enjoy genuine, year-round exposure across multiple channels:

  • Match day presence — banners and signage at 9 home games each season, seen by players, supporters, and the broader community
  • Digital reach — promotion to 1,100+ Facebook followers, plus exposure on our website and social media channels
  • Club room visibility — logo displayed on the sponsors wall in our social club pavilion, seen at training, games, and functions throughout the year
  • Events and hospitality — tickets to club events including Season Launch, Sponsors Day, and Presentation Night
  • Community connection — align your business with a genuine grassroots sporting club that gives back to the local area
100% tax deductible. All sponsorship contributions to the Moorabbin Kangaroos Football Club are tax deductible.
