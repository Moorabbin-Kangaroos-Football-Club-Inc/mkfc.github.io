The Moorabbin Kangaroos (MKFC) website style should be based on the **North Melbourne Kangaroos** (AFL) website at [nmfc.com.au](https://www.nmfc.com.au/).

## Reference: North Melbourne Brand Elements

**1. Primary Color Palette:**
*   **Royal Blue (Primary):** Pantone 7687 C / Hex #1A3B8E / RGB (26, 59, 142) / CMYK (82, 59, 0, 44)
*   **White (Primary):** Hex #FFFFFF / RGB (255, 255, 255)
*   **Light Blue (Secondary):** Pantone 643 C / Hex #C4D1E5 / RGB (196, 209, 229) / CMYK (14, 9, 0, 10)

**2. NMFC Website Colors (nmfc.com.au):**
*   **Deep Navy Blue:** #01285e — used for header, navigation bar, and footer backgrounds
*   **White:** #FFFFFF — used for text on dark backgrounds and card/content backgrounds
*   **Light Gray:** used for secondary content area backgrounds

**3. Fonts/Typography:**
*   **Logo Font:** Custom, proprietary bold condensed sans-serif with distinctive angular cuts.
*   **Primary Brand Font (Digital/Text):** **DIN** or **Helvetica Neue** — clean, modern sans-serif for body text.
*   **Headline/Accent Font:** **DIN Condensed** or **Helvetica Neue Condensed** — bold, condensed sans-serif for prominent headings.

## NMFC Website Design Patterns to Follow

**Header/Navigation:**
*   Dark navy (#01285e) background with white text/logo
*   Club logo left-aligned
*   Horizontal primary navigation menu
*   Secondary navigation for key actions (e.g. membership, contact)

**Layout:**
*   Hero section with featured images/content and overlaid text
*   Card-based content grid for news and updates
*   Multi-column responsive layouts
*   Clean separation between content sections

**Footer:**
*   Dark navy background matching the header
*   Multi-column layout with links, social media icons, and sponsor logos
*   Social links: Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X (Twitter), TikTok

**UI Elements:**
*   Solid-colored call-to-action buttons
*   Content cards with image, title, and metadata
*   Hover states for interactive elements
*   Category badges and labels on content items

**Overall Aesthetic:**
*   Professional sports club presentation
*   Community-focused and inclusive
*   Modern, clean digital design
*   Strong institutional feel through navy/white colour scheme and bold typography
*   Emphasis on player content and fan/community engagement

## Summary for MKFC Implementation

*   **Primary Background/Accent:** Deep Navy Blue **#01285e** (header, nav, footer)
*   **Content Backgrounds:** White **#FFFFFF** with light gray for secondary sections
*   **Text on Dark:** White; **Text on Light:** Dark navy or near-black
*   **Secondary Accent:** Light Blue **#C4D1E5** for highlights and subtle accents
*   **Body/Text Font:** **DIN** or **Helvetica Neue** (sans-serif)
*   **Headline Font:** **DIN Condensed** or **Helvetica Neue Condensed** (bold)
*   **Design approach:** Follow the clean, card-based, navy-and-white aesthetic of nmfc.com.au
