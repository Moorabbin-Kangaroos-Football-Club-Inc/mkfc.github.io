# Register Your Interest

Thinking about playing footy with the **Moorabbin Kangaroos**? We'd love to hear from you. Whether you're a first-time player, returning to the game, or looking for a new club — there's a spot for you at MKFC.

We field teams across senior men's, reserves, and (where numbers allow) masters and women's grades. All abilities welcome.

## Where we play

**Widdop Crescent Oval**, 41 Widdop Crescent, Hampton East VIC 3188 — five minutes from Moorabbin Station.

Training: Tuesday and Thursday evenings during the season.

## What happens next

1. We'll reply within 24 hours.
2. You'll be invited to a training session at Widdop Crescent Oval — no pressure, just come and have a kick.
3. If it feels like a fit, we'll walk you through registration, fees, and gear.

## Bring a mate

Footy's better with friends. If you know someone who'd enjoy a run, share this page or pass on **info@mkfc.org.au**.
