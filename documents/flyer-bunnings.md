# PLAY FOOTY IN MOORABBIN

## Join the Moorabbin Kangaroos

**Local club. Local ground. Local mates.**

---

## Who we're looking for

- **New players** — never picked up a Sherrin? We'll teach you.
- **Returning players** — back yourself for one more season.
- **Current players** — looking for a new club with a great culture.
- **All ages, all abilities** — seniors, reserves, masters, women's.

---

## Where & when

**Widdop Crescent Oval**
41 Widdop Crescent, Hampton East
*5 minutes from Moorabbin Station*

**Training:** Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:30pm
**Games:** Saturdays through winter

---

## Why MKFC?

- A genuine community club, not just a team
- Strong social side — Friday night dinners at the clubrooms
- Pathways from juniors through to masters
- Family-friendly, multicultural, welcoming
