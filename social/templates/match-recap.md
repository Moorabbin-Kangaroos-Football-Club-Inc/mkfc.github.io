# Match Recap Template

Use Saturday evening or Sunday morning after a match. Fill in **Inputs**, then ask Claude to draft the three versions.

## Inputs

- **Round:** e.g. Round 5
- **Result (Seniors):** MKFC X.X (XX) def / def by [Opponent] X.X (XX)
- **Result (Reserves):** same format
- **Venue:** ground name, suburb
- **Goalkickers:** Seniors top goalkickers (name + count)
- **Best players:** Seniors best 3–5 (from coaches' votes if available)
- **Match moment:** one sentence — the turning point, a great mark, a debut, a milestone game
- **Injuries / notes:** anything material (optional)
- **Next week:** Round X opponent + venue
- **Sponsor of the round:** name (optional)

## Voice notes

- Win or lose, tone is proud and respectful. No sledging opponents or umpires.
- Lead with the result and the venue. Don't bury the score.
- Celebrate effort, not just wins — community footy is about the group.
- Always thank volunteers, sponsors, and the opposition where relevant.

## Facebook version (target 100–180 words)

Structure:
1. Result line: "Seniors fell short / saluted by X points against [Opponent] at [Venue]."
2. How the match unfolded: 2–3 sentences. Where did momentum shift?
3. Standouts: best players (3–5 names) and goalkickers.
4. Reserves result in one paragraph.
5. Thanks: volunteers, sponsor of the round, opposition.
6. Next week: round, opponent, venue.

## Instagram caption (target 50–100 words)

Structure:
1. Result, big and clear ("ROOS BY 18" or "TOUGH ONE AT [VENUE]").
2. 1–2 sentences on the story of the match.
3. Best on ground + leading goalkicker.
4. Next match teaser.
5. Hashtags: `#MKFC #GoRoos #SFNL #CommunityFooty` + opponent + round.

Image guidance:
- Score tile: royal blue `#013087` background, white DIN Condensed headline with final score, opponent logo small in corner if available.
- Action photo: high contrast, players visible in royal blue guernseys. Alt text required.

## Short / X / Story version (<280 chars)

Win: `🏉 ROOS BY [margin]. Seniors def [Opponent] X.X (XX) to X.X (XX) at [Venue]. Best: [Name 1], [Name 2], [Name 3]. Goals: [Name]. #MKFC #GoRoos`

Loss: `Tough one at [Venue]. [Opponent] X.X (XX) def Roos X.X (XX). Best: [Name 1], [Name 2], [Name 3]. Back at it next week. #MKFC #GoRoos`

## Pre-publish checklist

- [ ] Scores verified against the official sheet
- [ ] All names spelled correctly (especially debutants)
- [ ] Reserves result included
- [ ] Sponsor / volunteers thanked
- [ ] Alt text on every image
- [ ] No comment on umpires
