# Sponsor Shout-out Template

Use to thank a sponsor — at signing, mid-season, after a sponsored round, or as part of an end-of-year wrap. Fill in **Inputs**, then ask Claude to draft the three versions.

## Inputs

- **Sponsor name:** full business name
- **Tier:** Major / Principal / Match-day / Supporter / In-kind
- **What they do:** one-line description of the business
- **Location:** suburb / address / online
- **Years supporting MKFC:** e.g. "3rd season with the Roos" or "new for 2026"
- **What their support funds:** jumpers, ground hire, junior program, canteen gear, etc.
- **Owner / contact name:** if they want to be named
- **Call to action:** visit / book / quote "MKFC" for discount / follow their page
- **Links & handles:** website, Facebook, Instagram
- **Occasion:** signing, sponsored round, anniversary, end of season

## Voice notes

- Genuine thanks, not transactional. Sponsors keep community footy alive — say so.
- Lead with what they do for the club, not with a sales pitch for them.
- Name the owner / family if they're locals — relationships matter more than logos.
- Don't oversell. One clear CTA is better than three.
- Refer to the [brand guide](../../branding/index.md) — sponsor logo must not exceed the visual weight of the MKFC logo in any graphic.

## Facebook version (target 100–180 words)

Structure:
1. Opening thank-you line, naming the sponsor and tier.
2. What the business does + where (one or two sentences).
3. What their support funds at the club — be specific (e.g. "their backing covers the senior guernsey run for 2026").
4. Years with the club / personal note about the owner if appropriate.
5. Call to action: visit, book, follow, mention MKFC.
6. Closing line: "On behalf of every Roo, thank you." (or similar)
7. Tag the sponsor's Facebook page.

## Instagram caption (target 50–100 words)

Structure:
1. Hook: "Big thanks to [Sponsor] — [one-line descriptor]."
2. One sentence on what their support means for the club.
3. CTA: where to find them.
4. Tag handle.
5. Hashtags: `#MKFC #GoRoos #SponsorSpotlight #SupportLocal #CommunityFooty` + sponsor-relevant tag.

Image guidance:
- Co-branded tile: royal blue `#013087` background, white MKFC logo on the left, sponsor logo on the right, light blue `#C8D6F0` divider between them.
- Sponsor logo never larger than the MKFC kangaroo — see [brand guide § Sponsor placement](../../branding/index.md#sponsor-placement).
- For a photo post: owner / staff in front of their business, ideally holding a club scarf or guernsey. Alt text required.

## Short / X / Story version

`Cheers to [Sponsor] — [one-line descriptor] and proud backer of the Roos. Find them at [link / suburb]. #MKFC #GoRoos #SupportLocal`

## Pre-publish checklist

- [ ] Sponsor has approved the post, wording, and any photo of their staff / premises
- [ ] Business name spelled exactly as they use it (capitalisation, ampersands, Pty Ltd)
- [ ] Owner name confirmed and approved if used
- [ ] Sponsor logo file is the version they supplied (not screen-grabbed)
- [ ] Sponsor logo sized smaller than the MKFC logo in any graphic
- [ ] Tagged the correct Facebook / Instagram handle
- [ ] One clear CTA, with a working link
- [ ] Alt text on every image
