# Player Spotlight Template

A recurring feature post — one player, volunteer, or club identity per fortnight. Fill in **Inputs**, then ask Claude to draft the three versions.

## Inputs

- **Name:** full name
- **Role:** player (grade, position), coach, committee, volunteer, life member
- **Games / years at club:** approximate is fine
- **Background:** where they grew up / played junior footy / joined MKFC
- **Career highlight at MKFC:** best & fairest, premiership, milestone game, etc.
- **Off-field:** what they do outside footy — job, family, study
- **Why MKFC?:** one quote (1–2 sentences) on what the club means to them
- **Fun fact:** optional — a quirk teammates would recognise
- **Photo:** action shot or portrait in club gear

## Voice notes

- Warm, human, specific. Avoid generic "great clubman" filler — pull out one real detail.
- Get a direct quote from the player wherever possible.
- Use first name on second reference after introducing full name.
- Refer to the [brand guide](../../branding/index.md) for any graphic treatment.

## Facebook version (target 120–200 words)

Structure:
1. Headline: "Player Spotlight — [Name]" or "Meet the Roos: [Name]".
2. Snapshot: role, games/years at club.
3. Path to MKFC: 2–3 sentences on their background.
4. On-field: position, strengths, highlight.
5. Off-field: job/family/interests.
6. Quote from the player on what the club means to them.
7. Closing line: "Next time you're at [home ground], say g'day to [first name]."

## Instagram caption (target 60–100 words)

Structure:
1. Hook: "Meet [Name] — [one-line descriptor, e.g. 'centre half-back, 80 games, full-time sparky']."
2. Two-sentence story (joining MKFC + a highlight).
3. Pull-quote from the player.
4. CTA: "Full feature on Facebook." (if cross-posting)
5. Hashtags: `#MKFC #GoRoos #PlayerSpotlight #CommunityFooty #SFNL`.

Image guidance:
- Portrait tile: photo on the left, royal blue `#013087` panel on the right with white DIN Condensed name and role. Light blue `#C8D6F0` accent line allowed.
- Use the white/reversed logo on the royal blue panel.
- Alt text required — describe the person and setting.

## Short / X / Story version

`Meet [Name] — [role], [years/games at club]. "[Short quote]." Read the full story on Facebook. #MKFC #GoRoos`

## Pre-publish checklist

- [ ] Player has approved the post and photo
- [ ] Quote is verbatim, not paraphrased
- [ ] Name spelling and role confirmed
- [ ] Photo is in club gear or otherwise on-brand
- [ ] Alt text on every image
- [ ] Tagged the player's profile (with permission)
