# Match Preview Template

Use the Thursday or Friday before a match. Fill in **Inputs** below, then ask Claude (or a writer) to produce the three versions.

## Inputs

- **Round:** e.g. Round 5
- **Opponent:** full club name
- **Grade(s):** Seniors / Reserves / Under-19s / Women's
- **Venue:** ground name, suburb
- **Date & bounce times:** Saturday DD Month — Reserves H:MMpm, Seniors H:MMpm
- **Last meeting:** result + brief note (optional)
- **Form note:** one-liner on how MKFC is travelling (optional)
- **Players to watch:** 1–3 names (optional)
- **Sponsor of the round:** name (optional)
- **Call to action:** e.g. "Get down and back the Roos" / canteen open / volunteers needed

## Voice notes

- Confident, not cocky. Respect the opponent.
- Use "the Roos" or "Moorabbin" — not nicknames invented on the fly.
- Refer to the [brand guide](../../branding/index.md) for any graphic — royal blue background, white kangaroo, condensed headline.

## Facebook version (target 80–150 words)

Structure:
1. Opening line: round, opponent, where and when.
2. Context: form, last meeting, or what's at stake.
3. Names: 1–3 players to watch, coach quote if available.
4. Sponsor / community line.
5. Call to action + bounce times for both grades.

## Instagram caption (target 40–80 words)

Structure:
1. Hook line (short, punchy).
2. Where, when, who.
3. One line of intrigue (form / rivalry / milestone).
4. CTA.
5. Hashtags: `#MKFC #GoRoos #SFNL #CommunityFooty` + opponent + round.

Image guidance: matchday tile on royal blue `#013087` with white DIN Condensed headline ("ROUND X — vs OPPONENT"). Alt text required.

## Short / X / Story version (<280 chars)

`🏉 Round X — Roos vs [Opponent], [Venue], Sat [date]. Reserves H:MMpm, Seniors H:MMpm. Get down and back the boys in blue. #MKFC #GoRoos`

- [ ] Opponent name spelled correctly

- [ ] Alt text on every image
- [ ] Sponsor tagged (if mentioned)
