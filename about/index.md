# About Moorabbin Kangaroos Football Club

The Moorabbin Kangaroos Football Club was established in 1965 and competes in the Southern Football and Netball League (SFNL).

We are a community-focused club based at Widdop Crescent Oval in Hampton East, fielding Senior and Reserves teams in Division 4.

## Club History {#club-history}

### Founding Years

The club was established in 1965 by a group of local football enthusiasts who wanted to create a community-focused club in the Moorabbin area.

### Early Success

- **1972**: First Division 2 Premiership
- **1978**: Moved to current home ground at Widdop Crescent
- **1985**: Club record attendance of 2,500 for Grand Final

### Modern Era

- **2005**: Celebrated 40th anniversary
- **2012**: Joined Southern Football Netball League
- **2018**: Introduced women's team (currently inactive)
- **2020**: Survived COVID-19 challenges with strong membership

### Premierships

- **Division 2**: 1972, 1985, 1998
- **Division 3**: 2007
- **Reserves**: 2015
